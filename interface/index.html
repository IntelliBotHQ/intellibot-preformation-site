Operating model

Robust does not mean uncontrolled. Every surface has a safety role.

The interface direction emphasizes a complete trading-operations console: readiness checks, strategy validation, one-active-trade control, backtesting proof, risk supervision, reporting forensics, and configuration governance.

Visibility

State before action

Users should see whether IntelliBot is safe, ready, waiting, blocked, armed, or inactive before any trade path is considered.

Proof

Validation before eligibility

Backtesting, data integrity, and strategy proof are treated as prerequisites, not optional marketing labels.

Restraint

One active trade maximum

The planned cockpit keeps active exposure narrow so the risk event is easier to supervise, explain, and recover.

Evidence

Audit-ready decisions

Reporting and decision records are planned as first-class surfaces, not afterthoughts.

Concept gallery

Seven planned surfaces

Each image is presented as concept interface direction only. Values, balances, symbols, timestamps, win/loss indicators, scenarios, strategy names, and status labels are illustrative placeholders.

01 · Dashboard. The planned mission-control view for risk budget, readiness, market context, advisory context, session awareness, active-trade status, and decision logs.
02 · Live Trading. Candidate screener, chart context, active position state, execution controls, AI/news/sentiment context, and emergency controls.
03 · Strategies. Strategy library, proof status, market-regime fit, activation matrix, risk compatibility, and advisory compatibility.
04 · Backtesting. Validation runs, data integrity, proof status, slippage/fee assumptions, scenario comparison, drawdown/equity curves, and audit/export readiness.
05 · Risk Center. Risk profile lifecycle, daily/weekly drawdown, cooldown state, one-active-trade enforcement, guardrails, and safety-event history.
06 · Reporting. Trade history, decision forensics, linked evidence, export readiness, audit trail health, and explainable records.
07 · Settings. Exchange connection, API credential status, account selection, environment controls, risk profile configuration, advisory providers, security settings, retention controls, and readiness checks.
No performance claim: this gallery is not evidence of profitability, account value, win rate, strategy success, product readiness, or trading outcomes. The final software may differ materially after engineering, testing, security review, legal review, and user-feedback cycles.
