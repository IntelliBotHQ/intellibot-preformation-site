Interactive gallery

Seven planned surfaces. Click any panel to enlarge.

Each panel has been aligned to the approved IntelliBot emblem treatment. The gallery uses local JavaScript only: no analytics, no third-party scripts, no form capture, and no external dependencies.

01 · Dashboard. Mission-control direction for risk budget, readiness, market context, advisory context, active-trade state, and decision logs.
02 · Live Trading. Candidate screener, chart context, active position state, execution controls, advisory context, and emergency controls.
03 · Strategies. Strategy library, proof status, market-regime fit, activation matrix, risk compatibility, and advisory compatibility.
04 · Backtesting. Validation runs, data integrity, proof status, scenario comparison, equity curves, drawdown view, and audit/export readiness.
05 · Risk Center. Risk profile lifecycle, drawdown controls, cooldown state, one-active-trade enforcement, guardrails, and safety-event history.
06 · Reporting. Trade history, decision forensics, linked evidence, export readiness, audit trail health, and explainable records.
07 · Settings. Exchange connection, API credential status, account selection, environment controls, risk profile setup, advisory providers, security settings, and readiness checks.
No performance claim: this gallery is not evidence of profitability, account value, win rate, strategy success, product readiness, or trading outcomes. The final software may differ materially after engineering, testing, security review, legal review, and user-feedback cycles.
