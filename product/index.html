Product model · Local-alpha direction · Not released functionality

A trading-operations cockpit designed to limit authority before it automates.

IntelliBot is being designed as a local-first, non-custodial software framework for Coinbase Spot. The intended experience is not a signal feed or a profit dashboard; it is a controlled operating system for proof, risk, execution restraint, and decision evidence.

Market scopeCoinbase Spot first · long-only active trading
Exposure ruleMaximum one active trade at any time by default
Safety postureNo custody · no withdrawal permissions · sandbox before live
Commercial statusNo public product, beta enrollment, or investment offering on this site
Operating model

The product is structured as a control stack.

Each layer has a job. The system should not move from observation to live action unless proof, risk state, account safety, and operational controls are aligned.

Observe

Candidate and regime context

Market activity, session behavior, liquidity, and catalyst context inform the workflow without authorizing trades by themselves.

Prove

Strategy validation

Backtesting and proof status establish whether a strategy has earned eligibility inside the intended operating rules.

Gate

Risk and account safety

RiskManager, drawdown controls, cooldowns, and account safety checks constrain the system before execution.

Act

Controlled automation

Initial direction is Coinbase Spot, long-only, one active trade, no custody, and no withdrawal permission.

Explain

Decision records

Reporting and logs are planned as evidence surfaces so the user can inspect what happened and why.

AI / news / sentiment boundary

Advisory intelligence is not trade authority.

AI, sentiment, and news catalyst modules may summarize context. They do not open trades, increase risk, bypass safety systems, or create live eligibility.
