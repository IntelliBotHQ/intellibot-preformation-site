Product direction · Not released functionality

A trading cockpit built around restraint.

IntelliBot is intended to support supervised crypto automation with strong risk controls, strategy validation, and explainable decisions.

AreaIntended initial direction
Exchange scopeCoinbase spot-first execution in a constrained initial market surface.
Trading directionLong-only active trading; no shorting, margin, leverage, futures, or derivatives in the initial release direction.
Active exposureOne active position at a time by default, positioned as Focused Risk Mode.
CustodyNon-custodial software direction; users retain their exchange accounts and funds.
PermissionsNo withdrawal permissions in the intended exchange API configuration.
Strategy proofBacktesting, scenario review, sandbox validation, and readiness gates before possible live eligibility.
AI roleAdvisory context only; AI is not intended to place trades or bypass safety controls.
Important: No product is currently offered through this website. Descriptions are roadmap-level product direction and may change before any beta or commercial release.
