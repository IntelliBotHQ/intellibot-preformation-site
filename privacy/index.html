Interim privacy notice

Minimal data collection by design.

This interim deployment intentionally avoids public enrollment forms and optional analytics while the project remains pre-formation.

Information you choose to send

If you email info@intellibot.us, the information contained in your email will be received and processed for purposes of responding to your inquiry.

Do not send credentials, wallet keys, account secrets, or sensitive financial information.

Website technology

This interim site is not intentionally configured with a beta-signup form, advertising pixel, or optional website analytics. Standard technical or security logs may be processed by the hosting provider as part of operating and protecting the site.

This notice must be updated before forms, analytics, downloads requiring registration, or beta intake are enabled.
