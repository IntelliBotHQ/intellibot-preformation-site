Roadmap · Phase-gated · Not a product release schedule

Roadmap discipline: prove the system before expanding the system.

The roadmap is intentionally gated. IntelliBot should not move toward live operation, beta enrollment, analytics, provider integrations, or commercial workflows until the relevant security, schema, QA, and compliance gates are satisfied.

0
Current

Phase 0 · TPMS and schema-readiness control

TT169 / V2.8 remains the governing baseline for migration readiness, one-active-trade enforcement, drawdown persistence, proof placeholders, and stale metadata cleanup.

1
Next only after gates

Phase 1 · Controlled implementation readiness

Runtime work remains dependent on TPMS authority, Codex Plan-mode control, dependency governance, QA review, and validated migration planning.

2
Later

Private validation and sandbox proving

Strategy proof, sandbox behavior, reporting, risk controls, and operator workflows must be validated before any public beta process.

3
Future

Public beta and commercial pathway review

Any beta enrollment, analytics activation, investor communications, or commercial workflow requires separate approval and compliance review.

Roadmap boundary: Dates, features, and sequencing remain subject to engineering, testing, security review, legal review, and funding decisions. Nothing on this page is a product availability commitment.
