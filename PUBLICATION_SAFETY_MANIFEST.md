## V5 product and roadmap update

This package updates the Product and Roadmap pages from the prior V4 snapshot to provide a more complete public-safe description of the current IntelliBot product direction. The update aligns public copy with the TT-169 V2.8 baseline while preserving the pre-formation, no-offering, no-beta, no-performance-claim publication perimeter.

Updated public-facing areas:

- Product overview: risk-first architecture, Coinbase Spot / long-only boundary, one-active-trade model, strategy proof, backtesting, sandbox gating, advisory intelligence limits, UI surfaces, structural entry model, exit hardening, and engineering-readiness posture.
- Roadmap: phase-gated progression from Phase 0 governance and schema readiness through foundation, signal spine, risk/backtesting, live-execution hardening, cockpit UI, advisory context, and later commercialization/compliance review.

Schema hardening, audit readiness, migration readiness, and database integrity language are presented as engineering readiness only and not as regulatory certification, security certification, audit attestation, broker-dealer compliance, investment-adviser compliance, or custody certification.

## Language verification

The public pages state that IntelliBot is a pre-formation product initiative in development, do not represent a released trading product, do not solicit an investment, do not claim performance, and do not accept beta enrollment at this interim stage.
