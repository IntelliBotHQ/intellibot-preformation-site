Trust & Safety · Product direction

Safety is not a footer. It is the operating model.

IntelliBot is being scoped around deterministic boundaries: no custody, no withdrawals, one-active-trade control, sandbox-first validation, and advisory intelligence that cannot override risk systems.

No custody

User-controlled accounts

The intended product does not hold user funds or operate pooled accounts.

No withdrawals

Trading permission only

The intended API posture excludes withdrawal authority.

One active trade

Constrained exposure

The default design keeps active exposure narrow and easier to supervise.

Sandbox first

Validation before live

Sandbox and proof gates precede any live-capital eligibility.

Risk controls

Deterministic guardrails

Drawdown, cooldown, and safety controls are planned as hard gates.

AI advisory only

No override authority

AI/news/sentiment context cannot open trades, raise risk, or bypass safety systems.

Credential posture

API safety is part of product design.

The intended product direction is to keep exchange credentials close to the user, restrict permissions, verify account selection, and prevent high-risk permission scopes from becoming part of the workflow.

Public-site boundary

No API keys, wallet keys, exchange credentials, or account access are collected through this website.
