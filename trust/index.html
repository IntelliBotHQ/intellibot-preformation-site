Trust & safety

Safety boundaries are part of the product.

IntelliBot is designed around constraints that reduce unnecessary risk and make decisions reviewable.

Planned product safeguards

  • Non-custodial direction and no pooled capital.
  • No withdrawal permissions in the intended configuration.
  • Mandatory safety controls and explicit arming before live activity.
  • Backtest and sandbox evidence before live eligibility.
  • Secret-redacted, accountable lifecycle reconstruction as a design objective.

Never send secrets through this site

Do not email or submit exchange API keys, API secrets, authentication tokens, wallet private keys, recovery phrases, or passwords to IntelliBot through this website or by email.

Security status: Credential protection and audit-readiness features are development requirements, not claims of deployed production security or regulatory certification.
