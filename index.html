Product thesis

An automation cockpit built around control layers, not black-box trading.

The planned product is organized around a disciplined operating model: scan, validate, gate, execute, supervise, and record. The purpose is not to promise performance; it is to make the trade path harder to misuse.

01

Market context

Candidate discovery and market-state awareness without treating signals as permission to trade.

02

Strategy proof

Backtesting, scenario review, and proof status before a strategy can be trusted.

03

Risk gate

Position limits, drawdown state, cooldowns, one-active-trade control, and safety checks.

04

Execution restraint

Long-only, Coinbase Spot-first automation with no withdrawal permission and no custody.

05

Decision evidence

Logs, rationale, reporting, and exportable records for review and improvement.
