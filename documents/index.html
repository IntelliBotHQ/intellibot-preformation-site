What the guide covers

Product detail without expanding the public claim boundary.

The guide gives a deeper explanation than the landing pages while preserving the same risk-first, pre-formation, no-performance-claim posture.

Scope

What IntelliBot is

Coinbase Spot, long-only active trading, one active trade maximum, non-custodial API use, and no withdrawal permissions.

Users

Who it is for

Beginner, intermediate, and advanced retail users, plus design partners and interested stakeholders evaluating product discipline.

Roadmap

Phase 0 through Phase 7

Customer-readable roadmap bands covering alpha governance, local foundation, proof gates, live-trading cockpit, reporting, release hardening, and commercialization review.

Workflow

UI and operator flow

Dashboard, Live Trading, Strategies, Backtesting, Risk Center, Reporting, and Settings, with retired standalone Screener/Charts/AI pages embedded into the correct surfaces.

Proof

Backtesting and guardrails

Risk profiles, proof gates, market universe controls, canonical backtesting, and objective validation requirements before trust.

Trust

AI, security, and audit integrity

AI/news/sentiment advisory limits, local credential posture, no recovery backdoor, API permission limits, and audit-linked decision evidence.
